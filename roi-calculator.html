What is ROI?

ROI (Return on Investment) shows how much profit you earned relative to what you invested. It’s a simple way to compare projects, marketing campaigns, equipment purchases, and other business decisions.

ROI Formula

Net Profit = Return − Investment Cost
ROI % = (Net Profit ÷ Investment Cost) × 100

How to Use ROI in a Small Business

  • Compare marketing channels (ads, SEO, referrals)
  • Evaluate new equipment or software purchases
  • Prioritize projects with higher expected returns
  • Track performance over time
