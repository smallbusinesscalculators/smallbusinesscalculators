How to convert margin and markup

Margin and markup are related, but they are not the same. Margin is based on selling price. Markup is based on cost.

Margin to markup:

Markup % = (Margin % / (100 - Margin %)) × 100

Markup to margin:

Margin % = (Markup % / (100 + Markup %)) × 100

Example: a 44% margin equals a 78.57% markup.

Why this matters

Many small business owners mix up margin and markup when setting prices. This can lead to lower profits than expected. Using the correct percentage helps you quote accurately and protect profitability.
