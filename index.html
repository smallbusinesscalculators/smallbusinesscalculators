  • Profit Margin is based on revenue (selling price).
  • Markup is based on cost.
  • Break-even tells you when you cover total costs.
  • ROI compares profit to investment cost.

FAQ

What’s the difference between margin and markup?

Margin is profit divided by revenue. Markup is profit divided by cost. They are not the same percentage.

Which calculator should I use for pricing?

Start with Profit Margin if you have a selling price, or Markup if you want to set a selling price from your cost.

Can I use these for taxes or accounting?

These tools are for quick business math and planning. For taxes/accounting, confirm figures with your accountant.
