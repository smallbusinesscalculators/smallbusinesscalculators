What Is Net Profit?

Net profit (also called net income) is the amount of money left after subtracting all business expenses from revenue. This includes operating expenses, taxes, interest, and overhead.

Net Profit Formula

Net Profit = Revenue − Total Expenses
Net Profit Margin = Net Profit ÷ Revenue × 100

Gross Profit vs Net Profit

Gross profit subtracts only cost of goods sold. Net profit subtracts all expenses. See also: Gross Margin Calculator and Profit Margin Calculator.
