About SmallBusinessCalculators

SmallBusinessCalculators.com provides free, practical calculators and guides for small business owners, entrepreneurs, freelancers, and anyone making business decisions.

The goal of this site is simple: make important business calculations easier to understand and faster to use. Instead of relying on complicated spreadsheets or rough mental estimates, you can use straightforward tools to calculate profit margin, markup, break-even point, ROI, net profit, contribution margin, and more.

Each calculator is designed to be simple, fast, and useful in real business situations. The site also includes guides that explain the formulas and concepts behind the numbers so you can understand the result, not just generate it.

What This Site Offers

  • Free calculators for pricing, profitability, and financial planning.
  • Simple explanations of business metrics and formulas.
  • Tools designed to work well on desktop and mobile.
  • Practical resources for small business decision-making.

Who These Calculators Are For

SmallBusinessCalculators.com is built for people who need clear answers without unnecessary complexity. That includes:

  • Small business owners
  • Freelancers and consultants
  • Ecommerce sellers
  • Service businesses
  • Anyone comparing pricing, costs, margins, and profitability

What We Focus On

The site focuses on the core business math that matters most in everyday decision-making. That includes:

  • Profit Margin Calculator
  • Markup Calculator
  • Margin vs Markup Calculator
  • Break-Even Calculator
  • ROI Calculator
  • Net Profit Calculator
  • Contribution Margin Calculator
  • Profitability Guide

Our Goal

The goal is to build a useful, trustworthy resource that helps people make better business decisions with less confusion. Financial tools should be accessible, practical, and easy to use. This site is designed around that idea.

The calculators and guides are intended for education and planning. They can help you estimate, compare, and understand business numbers more clearly, but they do not replace accounting, tax, legal, or financial advice tailored to your situation.
