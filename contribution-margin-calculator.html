What Is Contribution Margin?

Contribution margin shows how much each sale contributes toward covering fixed costs and generating profit. It is a key metric used in break-even analysis and pricing decisions.

Contribution Margin Formula

Contribution Margin (per unit) = Price − Variable Cost
Contribution Margin % = Contribution Margin ÷ Price × 100

Why Contribution Margin Matters

  • Determines break-even point
  • Helps with pricing strategy
  • Shows product profitability
  • Improves financial planning
