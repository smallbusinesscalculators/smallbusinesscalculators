Profit Margin Formula

Profit = Revenue − Cost
Profit Margin % = Profit ÷ Revenue × 100
Markup % = Profit ÷ Cost × 100

Example: Revenue $100 and cost $60 gives a profit of $40 and a profit margin of 40%.

Profit Margin vs Markup

Profit margin is based on revenue, while markup is based on cost. That means the same numbers can produce different percentages. Understanding both helps you price correctly and avoid losing profit.

You can also compare the two using our Free Margin vs Markup Calculator.
