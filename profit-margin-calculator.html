What is Profit Margin?

Profit margin measures how much of your revenue remains as profit after covering costs. It is one of the most important metrics for small businesses because it shows how efficiently you turn sales into profit.

Profit Margin Formula

Profit = Revenue − Cost
Gross Margin % = Profit ÷ Revenue × 100
Markup % = Profit ÷ Cost × 100

Why Profit Margin Matters

Understanding your gross margin helps you:

  • Price products correctly
  • Identify cost issues
  • Improve profitability
  • Compare performance over time

Margin vs Markup – What's the Difference?

Many business owners confuse margin and markup. Margin is based on revenue, while markup is based on cost. A 50% markup does not equal a 50% margin.
